AUTO COMPONENTS MANUFACTURING UNIT(Mini project)
➔ It is the replication of the automobile components supplier unit, which
involves hierarchical divisional working(Head, Stock Manager,
Shiftwise managers, and employees) and a shopping portal for the
automobile industry to purchase.
➔ Management of raw input supply and production factors.Division of
labour and wages among the workers. Different access is provided to
users based on their level. Urge in demand of product mechanism.
➔ Language: Python, Tkinter, MySQL.
